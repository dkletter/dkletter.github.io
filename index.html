Hi there! I’m Dan Kletter.

I help people make better informed decisions. Having spent the past 15 years in sales operations as a data analyst, I have developed a strong understanding of what data can tell us, and how to use it to communicate solutions that drive results.

In my spare time, I’m an obsessive music collector, freeform radio DJ at KFJC, and a great community enabler.

Since joining KFJC in 1993, I have booked, produced, and hosted countless live, on-air performances. I run 24 Hour Drone with The Norman Conquest. My acclaimed radio show, Psychoacoustic Soundclash, ran from 2000 to 2008.
